How Can I Improve myself?

How Can I Improve the World?

From the way we value our people, to the way we do business, and the way we live our values, SilverOak is exceptional.

We know how important your career is to you. It’s important to us too! That’s why we have a clear and transparent career path with international opportunities, supported by our industry-renowned training and development.

Each company has unique needs - and the talent solutions we offer cover everything that will cater to your specific needs.

Our distributed team framework accelerates the time to operate and speeds up
a time to value by simplifying establishing and managing global teams.

What do most people find rewarding about the role?

Negotiating on behalf of our clients to secure them the best candidates for their roles, and the best package for our candidates is challenging and rewarding.
Client and candidate satisfaction are high on the list of rewards for our consultants. We’re helping to change people’s lives.

Is recruitment a sales based role?

Yes, it's business-to-business sales.

What is the working environment like?

There is a constant buzz of activity. We have small business teams and the offices are open plan. In such a team-based organisation, it’s always busy, with lots of fun and everyone is highly supportive.

What do people love about the job?

The euphoria of placing candidates and being rewarded for achieving targets is hard to beat.
When it comes to SilverOak, pride in working for a well-known and respected brand is the icing on the cake.

What training is given in the first few months?

You’ll receive one to one support from your manager including at-desk coaching and local workshop sessions to fine tune your recruitment skills.

What is the selection process?

Successful applicants will be required to attend an interview,
along with the opportunity to spend some time with the team you will potentially join on the operating floor and a meeting with the relevant business MD.

What can I expect after attending an interview?

We will get in touch to confirm whether or not you have been successful, and provide you with relevant feedback.

Why work for SilverOak?

At SilverOak, you are part of a successful, established company at the leading edge of the recruitment industry.
We offer a rewarding corporate environment and create clear opportunities for our employees all over the world.
